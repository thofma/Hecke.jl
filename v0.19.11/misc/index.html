# saturate — Method.

saturate(A::ZZMatrix) -> ZZMatrix

Computes the saturation of , that is, a basis for , where is the row span of and the number of columns of .

Equivalently, return (minus zero rows) for an invertible rational matrix such that is integral and the elementary divisors of are all trivial.

Examples

julia> saturate(ZZ[1 2 3;4 5 6;7 0 7])
[1    2    3]
[1    1    1]
[0   -1   -1]

julia> saturate(ZZ[1 2 3;4 5 6;7 8 9])
[1   2   3]
[1   1   1]

julia> saturate(ZZ[1 2 3;4 5 6])
[1   2   3]
[1   1   1]

julia> saturate(ZZ[1 2;3 4;5 6])
[1   2]
[1   1]
