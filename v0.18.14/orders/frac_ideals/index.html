Fractional ideals

A fractional ideal in the number field is a -module such that there exists an integer which is an (integral) ideal in . Due to the Dedekind property of , the ideals for a multiplicative group.

Fractional ideals are represented as an integral ideal and an additional denominator. They are of type NfOrdFracIdl.
